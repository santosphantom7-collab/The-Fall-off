I’ve been thinking about everything… not just reacting, not just feeling… actually thinking. And yeah… I know I messed up. I hate that I hurt you. That part sits with me more than anything.
But there’s something I haven’t been able to shake… the silence. The way you can just go quiet… like I’m suddenly not there anymore.
I won’t lie to you… choosing silence over the hard conversations fucks me up. Because I would rather sit in something uncomfortable with you… argue, fix it, talk through it… than feel like I’m that easy to shut out.
Maybe we just handle things differently when it gets hard… I don’t always get it right, but I try to stay, to talk, to fix things instead of letting them drift. That’s just how I am.
I care about you more than I’ve probably said the right way. But I’m also learning something about myself right now… I can’t force you to talk to me. I can’t force you to choose me. So I won’t chase you.
Not because I don’t want you… but because I need to be someone who respects himself too.
You’re coming back on the 20th… And I won’t lie… I hope I get to see you. Not to argue… not to force anything… just… to look at you and know if there’s still something here.
If love was loud, I’d shout your name,
but mine has learned to stay the same.

Not in the noise, not in the chase,
but in the quiet you can’t replace.

I stayed… even when you pulled apart.

And maybe that’s what you’ll one day see,
no one will hold you the way I did… quietly.
